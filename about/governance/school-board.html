About the School Board

The School Board is the supreme governing body of Blessed Nursery & Primary School, responsible for policy formulation, strategic direction, and oversight of the institution's operations. Our board members bring diverse expertise and unwavering commitment to excellence in education.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Provide strategic leadership and direction for the school
  • Approve policies and regulations governing the school
  • Oversee financial management and resource allocation
  • Ensure quality education and academic standards
  • Monitor and evaluate school performance
  • Appoint and evaluate senior management staff
  • Foster partnerships with stakeholders and the community
  • Ensure compliance with educational regulations and standards

Board Composition

The School Board comprises distinguished members from various sectors including education, business, and community leadership, all committed to the advancement of quality education at Blessed Nursery & Primary School.

Board Chairperson

Provides overall leadership to the board and ensures effective governance and strategic oversight.

Legal Advisor

Ensures compliance with legal requirements and provides guidance on regulatory matters.

Finance Committee

Oversees financial planning, budgeting, and ensures fiscal responsibility and sustainability.

Academic Committee

Monitors academic standards, curriculum development, and educational quality assurance.

Community Representatives

Ensures community engagement and represents stakeholder interests in board decisions.

Infrastructure Committee

Oversees facilities development, maintenance, and infrastructure planning.

Board Meetings

The School Board meets regularly to discuss and make decisions on matters affecting the school's operations and development. Meeting schedules and minutes are available upon request from the school administration office.

Blessed Nursery & Primary School is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation for the betterment of society.
