Academic System & Intakes

Overview of our programs, terms, and assessment schedule

Child-Centered Learning Programs

We offer age-appropriate and skill-driven educational programs for nursery and primary levels.

Term System and Intakes

We operate a three-term system with intakes in January, May, and September for both nursery and primary children.

Assessments & Progress Reports

Children receive continuous assessment and progress reports throughout the year, with formal assessments at the end of each term.

Our Journey Through The Years

Milestones that shaped our institution

2021

Foundation

Established on 1st March 2021 after the first COVID-19 lockdown by Bamwenda John and Kambale Shadrack to serve rural children in Hanga 2 village.

2022

Sports Achievement

Achieved 2nd position in football competition tournament out of 12 school teams, recognizing and developing children's talents.

2024

Growth & Recognition

Currently serving 592 learners (352 girls, 240 boys) from Nursery to Primary 7, recognized by Ministry of Education with EMIS number 2A014094.

2023

Community Support

Acquired land for school structures and established partnerships with sister schools for textbook support.

2025

Ongoing Excellence

Continuing to serve rural vulnerable children with 12 teaching staff and 3 non-teaching staff, providing quality education foundation.

Blessed Nursery & Primary School is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation for the betterment of society.
